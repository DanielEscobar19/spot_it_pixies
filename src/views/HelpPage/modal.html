Game Rules

Place one card face down in the center of the table and deal the rest of the cards face down among the players. All players at the same time will try to find a pair of repeating symbols. If you find the symbol you say it out loud and discard it in the central pile. This will be the card you will use to search for the next symbol. The game continues until one of the players runs out of cards. The first to do so wins the game.
